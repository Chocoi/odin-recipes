Pizza Sauce

Description:

This sauce is best used in pizza, it will add a good flavor to pizza with a kick to it.

The sauce can also be used as a sauce in other things if you so desire.

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup of water
  • 1/4 cup of Vinegar
  • 1 egg
  • 1tsp of mustard
  • 1 Garlic clove
  • A bit of fresh Parsely
  • 1/2 cup of arbol chiles
  • salt to taste
  • Half a liter of oil

  1. Add the water,vinergar,salt,egg, mustard, garlic clove,fresh parsely, and arbol chiles to the blenderand blend them at high speed for 1 minute until

    everything is well incorporated.

  2. while the mixer is still going, add the oil in a small string like stream. It will take time to add it all but this is what will make the sauce fluffy.
  3. After all the oil is mixed in you can let it blend for a few seconds later and then it will be ready to serve!
