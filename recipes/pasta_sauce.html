Pasta Sauce

Home

Description:

If you have some extra time on your hands, this pasta sauce is perfect for you.

It takes about 2 hours to make but it will be worth the time.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 56oz of peeled tomatoes
  • 1 whole onion
  • 2tsp salt
  • 1tsp dried oregano
  • 1tsp dried basil
  • 1 Bay leaf
  • 1/4cup of basil
  • salt to taste
  • sugar to taste
  • pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Add 1/4 cup of olive oil to a sauce pan along with 2 garlic cloves
  2. Stir until the cloves are a bit toasted, and then add the 56oz of peeled tomatoes
  3. Crush the tomatoes by hand with a wooden spooon, this helps them cook quicker
  4. Once it starts to boil, add a whole onion cut in half along with 2tsp of salt
  5. Turn down the heat and let it simmer for 1 hour
  6. After one hour, discard some of the onion but leave some in
  7. Filter out the seeds with a food mill, after you're done put the sauce without the seeds back in the pan
  8. Add 1tsp of dried oregano, 1tsp of dried basil, and 1 Bay leaf to the pot, stir and let it simmer for 90 minutes
  9. After it's done simmering, remove the bay leave and add salt to taste, sugar to taste, black pepper to taste and 1/4 cups of fresh basil
  10. Stir again and it's ready to serve!
